WG1 Kickoff & Draft Scope

July 15, 2025

Welcome to the kickoff of Working Group 1 (WG1) of the Smart Mobility Security Standardization Forum. This announcement outlines the initial scope, objectives, and next steps for our collaborative effort to establish comprehensive security standards for smart mobility systems.

Meeting Overview

The WG1 kickoff meeting will take place on July 22, 2025, at 2:00 PM UTC via virtual conference. All registered participants will receive meeting details via email.

Draft Scope

WG1 will focus on the following key areas:

  • Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) Security: Establishing protocols for secure communication between vehicles, infrastructure, and cloud services
  • Authentication & Identity Management: Developing robust identity verification systems for connected vehicles and users
  • Data Privacy & Protection: Creating frameworks for protecting sensitive mobility data throughout its lifecycle
  • Incident Response: Defining standardized procedures for security incident detection, reporting, and mitigation

Agenda

  • Welcome & introductions (15 minutes)
  • SMSSF mission and objectives overview (20 minutes)
  • WG1 draft scope discussion (45 minutes)
  • Timeline & deliverables planning (30 minutes)
  • Next steps & action items (15 minutes)
  • Q&A session (15 minutes)

Participation

We encourage active participation from all stakeholders, including automotive manufacturers, technology providers, research institutions, and regulatory bodies. Your expertise and insights are crucial for developing effective and practical security standards.
