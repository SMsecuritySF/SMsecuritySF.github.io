Public Review Window Open

September 1, 2025

We are pleased to announce that the public review period for our draft smart mobility security standards is now open. This is an important opportunity for the broader community to provide feedback and help shape the future of mobility security.

Review Documents

The following draft standards are available for public review:

  • SMSSF-001: Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) Security Framework
  • SMSSF-002: Connected Vehicle Authentication Protocols
  • SMSSF-003: Mobility Data Privacy and Protection Guidelines
  • SMSSF-004: Security Incident Response Procedures

Review Period

The public review period runs from September 1, 2025, to October 15, 2025. All feedback must be submitted by 11:59 PM UTC on the closing date to be considered for incorporation into the final standards.

How to Participate

To access the draft documents and submit feedback:

  1. Register for a free reviewer account at review.smssf.org
  2. Download the draft standards documents
  3. Submit your comments using our structured feedback form
  4. Participate in optional virtual discussion sessions (schedule available on the review portal)

Feedback Guidelines

We welcome all constructive feedback, including:

  • Technical accuracy and completeness
  • Implementation feasibility
  • Clarity and usability of guidelines
  • Potential gaps or missing considerations
  • Alignment with existing industry practices

Your participation in this review process is crucial for developing robust, practical, and widely-adopted security standards for the smart mobility ecosystem.
