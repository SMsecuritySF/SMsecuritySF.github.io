Call for Contributions: Threat Taxonomy

August 2, 2025

The Smart Mobility Security Standardization Forum is developing a comprehensive threat taxonomy for smart mobility systems. We invite security researchers, industry practitioners, and domain experts to contribute their knowledge and experience to this critical initiative.

Project Overview

The threat taxonomy will serve as a foundational resource for:

  • Identifying and categorizing security threats specific to smart mobility ecosystems
  • Providing a common language for discussing mobility security risks
  • Supporting risk assessment and mitigation planning processes
  • Informing the development of security standards and best practices

Contribution Areas

We are seeking contributions in the following areas:

  • Threat Identification: Novel or emerging threats to connected and autonomous vehicles
  • Attack Vectors: Detailed analysis of potential attack methods and entry points
  • Impact Assessment: Evaluation of threat consequences on safety, privacy, and operations
  • Mitigation Strategies: Proven or proposed countermeasures and defensive techniques
  • Real-world Cases: Documented incidents and lessons learned from actual security events

Submission Guidelines

Contributions should be submitted by September 15, 2025 through our secure portal. All submissions will be reviewed by our expert panel and incorporated into the taxonomy with appropriate attribution.

For detailed submission guidelines and access to the contribution portal, please visit our contribution platform.
